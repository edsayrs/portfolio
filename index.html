Photo editing:

Chanin Art Advisory

From the spring of 2010 until the beginning of 2013 I worked remotely as a photo retoucher for Kimberly Chanin, principle at Chanin art advisory. The majority of my work involved superimposing photos of prospective pieces into specific spaces desired by the client. The purpose of this was to simulate how that piece would look in different lighting, backdrops, framing and other variables.
